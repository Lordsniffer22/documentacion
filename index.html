Getting Started

To get started on Adskit as a publisher(Content Creator), you need to sign up first.

Advertisers do not have to create any accounts with Adskit (You are free to place your Ads directly from any Telegram Account).

Commands

Make use of these commands while interracting with our Telegram Bot

  • /start: Initiate a conversation with Adskit bot.
  • /help: This will show you the usage guide and a list of available commands you can use in case you forget.
  • /register: This will open up a registration form for you to sign up as a Publisher/Content Creator
  • /done: Send this command only if you are done making a tiktok video and you want to submit it for review & then get paid.

Hot Keys

Our Bot is so sensitive to your messages. When doing some tasks like submitting a proof of payment or sending your Advert content, Make use of these hot Keys.

  • #paid: Use this as a starting line/word of your photo caption.
    The next line should also contain the Order ID the Bot gave you on submitting Your Ad content.
  • #Adcontent: Use this hot key as a starting word/line of your Ad content. If the Advert is Text only, It should start with #Adcontent. The same still applies to photo/video captions.

Adskit ID

We introduced the use of Adskit ID's which are the unique identifiers of each tiktoker who registered with us and is approved.
Adskit ID for each tiktoker is displayed in the TikTok Spaces channel on telegram. A tiktoker has to place this ID on their Bio details and should stay visible. It allows advertisers be sure of what they are paying for.

How the platform Works

Our Platform uses a friendly bot that manages both a TikTok content creator and Advertisers.
== [Advertisers] to [AdsKit] & finally to [TikToker] ==

#1. An advertiser Requests to have his/her advert placed in a TikToker's next video. The bot inboxes him/her with the instructions on how to submit the Advert to Adskit.

#2. Adskit reviews the Advert upon recieving it and if the advert meets the standards, an Ad placement request is sent to the Tiktoker informing him/her of the deal.

#3. A user has the right to accept or Decline the Ad placement request. If he/she accepts to take the deal, the TikToker is expected to make a video in any way he likes, making sure the Advert is included in the video.

#4. The Tiktoker then submits a link to the already uploaded video to Adskit. If we confirm the advert is there as the advertiser asked, we then make a payment to the tiktoker's account.
